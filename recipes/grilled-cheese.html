Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

A delicious grilled cheese sandwich made with buttered texas toast and thick slices of American and Proovolone cheeses.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Butter one side of 2 pieces of bread.
  2. Place one slice of bread butter side down in frying pan.
  3. Place cheese on unbuttered side.
  4. Place other slice of bread butter side up on top.
  5. Fry until golden then flip and repeat.
