Taco Recipe

Description

White people tacos featuring decadent seasoned ground beef. Topped with a mountain of cheese, sour cream, and lettuce. All wrapped inside a flour tortilla.

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortilla
  • Seasoned Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Lay tortilla flat on plate.
  2. Put desired amount of ground beef on center of tortilla.
  3. Add desired amount of cheese.
  4. Add desired amount of sour cream, and lettuce.
  5. Roll tortilla.
